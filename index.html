About Safar Dil Se

Our story, our passion, our commitment to creating unforgettable journeys

Founded by Jeet & Shubham – Passionate Travelers with a Purpose

Safar Dil Se is more than just a travel company — it’s a heartfelt initiative born from the love of exploration and community. Founded by Jeet and Shubham, two passionate travelers driven by the idea of creating soulful and memorable experiences, our mission is to make travel accessible, meaningful, and truly unforgettable. We specialize in curating well-organized, budget-friendly trips for all age groups. Whether you're a young explorer seeking adventure or someone looking for a peaceful escape, we ensure every journey is packed with emotion, discovery, and connection. Our motto — "Jiyo Khul Ke" — reflects our belief that life should be lived freely, fully, and with joy. Every itinerary, destination, and detail is crafted with that in mind.

Our Mission

To curate soulful, well-organized, and budget-friendly trips for young explorers who want to experience India's most beautiful destinations while forming lifelong friendships and creating unforgettable memories.
